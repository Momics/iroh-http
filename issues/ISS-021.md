# [ISS-021] Clippy hard gate currently fails

## Summary

`cargo clippy --workspace -- -D warnings` fails on redundant closure warnings in `iroh-http-core`. The project's principles require a lint-clean hard gate.

## Evidence

- `crates/iroh-http-core/src/client.rs:130` — redundant closure
- `crates/iroh-http-core/src/client.rs:461` — redundant closure
- `crates/iroh-http-core/src/lib.rs:234` — redundant closure
- `docs/principles.md:286` — requires lint-clean hard gate

## Impact

CI/lint gate is broken relative to the documented quality standard. New contributors may not notice the violations.

## Remediation

1. Replace the redundant closures with direct function pointer references at those three sites.

## Acceptance criteria

1. `cargo clippy --workspace -- -D warnings` passes with no warnings.
